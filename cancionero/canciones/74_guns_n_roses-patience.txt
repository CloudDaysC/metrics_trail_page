This is how I play the song at my solo acoustic shows. When we do a full show, the other
guitarist plays the First and Third solos, because I don't know them, so they wont be
tabbed. However, the song sounds very natural with out either of them. Just go right 
into the lyrics and skip that part.
**Tuned Half-Step Down**

**Note** When Strumming, Accents can make or break this song. It's important to listen
to the track to get it down.
